Our Story

A Dream Brewed to Perfection

What started as a small coffee cart in 2019 has blossomed into one of Mumbai's most beloved café destinations.

Brew Bliss Café was founded by two friends, Raj and Priya, who shared a passion for exceptional coffee and warm hospitality. Their vision was simple: create a space where people could escape the city's chaos and find comfort in a perfect cup of coffee.

Today, that vision has grown into a thriving community hub where friends meet, professionals work, and families celebrate. Every cup we serve carries the same dedication and love that started it all.

"Coffee is not just a drink; it's a moment of peace in a busy world."

Our Mission & Vision

What drives us every single day

Our Mission

To create memorable experiences through exceptional coffee, delicious food, and genuine hospitality. We believe in serving our community with integrity, sustainability, and a whole lot of love.

Our Vision

To be recognized as Mumbai's premier café destination, known for our commitment to quality, innovation in culinary arts, and creating a welcoming space for everyone who walks through our doors.

Our Journey

Milestones that shaped Brew Bliss

2019

The Beginning

Brew Bliss started as a small coffee cart at the local farmers market, serving just 5 varieties of coffee.

2020

First Café

Opened our first brick-and-mortar location on MG Road, expanding our menu to include breakfast items.

2022

Award Winner

Named "Best Café in Mumbai" by the City Food Awards. Our team grew to 25 passionate individuals.

2024

Expansion

Launched our second location and introduced our signature roasting facility.

2025

Today

Continuing to serve our community with the same passion and dedication that started it all.
